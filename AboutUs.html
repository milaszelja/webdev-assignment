    About Us

    Burning Grill is an exquisite concept of a restaurant and lounge Bar. Located in the heart of Palm Jumeirah in Zabeel Saray Hotel With magnificent view on Jumeirah beach coastline, on our outside terrace you may enjoy every sunset that is bringing the promise of a new dawn. Our venue will be your new favorite escape where you will feel the unique love of art and creation. Allow us to take you on a journey of awakening your senses. Your new home with sophisticated and antique interior design. Enjoy our bar menu drinks inspired by Leonardo Da Vinci life. Along with Mediterranean cuisine filled with aromas and taste with Asian touch. Choose in between our many unique theme nights that will give you goosebumps.
